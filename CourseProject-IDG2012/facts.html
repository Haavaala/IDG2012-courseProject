Facts

Fredrikstad is a municipality in the Østfold region in Viken county in Eastern Norway in Norway. It has 84 414 inhabitants. It is thus Østfold's largest, Eastern Norway's third largest city and Norway's seventh largest municipality in terms of population.

Fredrikstad is often characterized as Norway's first Renaissance city, with quarters bounded by streets laid out in a regular pattern. Fredrikstad municipality's area is 559 square kilometers. It borders Råde in the north, Sarpsborg in the east and Hvaler in the south.
